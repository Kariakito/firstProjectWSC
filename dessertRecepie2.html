Dulce de Lechoza or Papaya

Ingredients

  • 2 kilos papaya, or very green milky
  • 2 kilos piloncillo, or paper
  • 1/2 kilos sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 leaves fig
  • cloves
  • water
    •

    Instructions


    Peel the milky and remove the seeds, cut into thin strips.
    Place on a tray, sprinkle with baking soda and let it rest for about 12 hours.
    In a pot place the strips of milky and add water to cover completely, bring to a high heat until it boils, cover and cook for 3 minutes.
    Go through a colander and reserve.
    On the other hand, in a large pot add 3 liters of water with the foil. Bring to a boil and cook for 15 minutes.
    Then strain with a cloth sieve and pour into the pot, add the cloves and fig leaves.
    Boil and cook uncovered until the syrup forms approximately 1 hour and a half.
    Remove from heat, let cool for about 30 minutes before cooling.
